Travel

Whoever you are: some evening take a step out of your house, which you know so well. Enormous space is near Rainer Maria Rilke

Rilke's quote is one of my favorites, and is quoted at the end of Michael Crichton's biographical novel Travels. Over the years, traveling and seeking out new experiences has become an important goal for me, and I try to keep it an on-going activity. Below are photographs from some of my favorite travel experiences. More to come.
